Washington Fire Data

This site attempts to visualize Washington fire data.

Fires in Washington may be fought by Federal agencies, State agencies, or local agencies, and the reporting is different for all, so data may be incomplete or inaccurate.

Source: https://gacc.nifc.gov/nwcc/information/fire_info.aspx

Source code: https://github.com/cocode/firedata

Data source: https://www.fire.ca.gov/incidents

Accuracy not guaranteed
