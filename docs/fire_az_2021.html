Arizona Fire Data

This site attempts to visualize Arizona fire data.

Fires in Arizona may be fought by Federal agencies, State agencies, or local agencies, and the reporting is different for all, so data may be incomplete or inaccurate.

Source: https://gacc.nifc.gov/swcc/predictive/intelligence/daily/UPLOAD_Files_toSWCC/YTD_10_INFORM_Protection_5_Website_Intell.csv

Source code: https://github.com/cocode/firedata

Data source: https://www.fire.ca.gov/incidents

Accuracy not guaranteed
